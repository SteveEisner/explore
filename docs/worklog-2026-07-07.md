## 2026-07-07 21:12 — Fixed: edits blanked the artifact panel mid-stream (Worker 1)

Root cause: `use-chat.ts` merged the *raw partial* edit patch into the committed program on every streamed token; `mergeStatements` replaced a valid statement mid-program with a truncated one whose unbalanced bracket/quote swallowed every statement after it (the partial HTML string even parsed as `<`/`>` BinOps), blanking the panel until the final `ui:spec` restored it. Verified by simulating token-by-token streaming through the real lang-core parser (single-statement edit, multi-statement patch, initial generation, deletion — no blank frames) plus 155 cut points across the benchmark .oui.
