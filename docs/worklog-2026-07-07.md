## 2026-07-07 21:12 — Fixed: edits blanked the artifact panel mid-stream (Worker 1)

Root cause: `use-chat.ts` merged the *raw partial* edit patch into the committed program on every streamed token; `mergeStatements` replaced a valid statement mid-program with a truncated one whose unbalanced bracket/quote swallowed every statement after it (the partial HTML string even parsed as `<`/`>` BinOps), blanking the panel until the final `ui:spec` restored it. Fix: new `mergeStreamingPatch` merges only the patch's *finished* statements (each section updates the moment its statement completes) and appends the unfinished tail at the end, where the streaming parser tolerates it — the old statement stays visible until its replacement is complete (first-definition-wins), and brand-new statements render progressively. Verified by simulating token-by-token streaming through the real lang-core parser (single-statement edit, multi-statement patch, initial generation, deletion — no blank frames) plus 155 cut points across the benchmark .oui.
